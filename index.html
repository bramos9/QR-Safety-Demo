What just happened?

You scanned a QR that pointed to a short URL. Short URLs hide the final destination which make it easier for attackers to trick user.

Link preview

Short URL: bit.ly/example-demo

Final URL: https://your-hosted-demo-page.example.edu/safe-info

How to check QR safety

  • Preview the link before opening (long-press on many phones or use a link preview app).
  • Look for HTTPS, domain spelling, and unexpected domains.
  • Don’t enter credentials or personal info into a suspicious link.
